PLACEMENT PROCESS

 3000+ Candidates Are Trained And Placed In Top MNC Under Our Guidance. Quastech feels proud to announce that more than 3000+ candidates are placed After getting trained from our Experienced Faculties. Our Goal is to place each and every candidate. Our skilled and expertise Placement Team have tie-up with various IT companies which helps us to achieve our goal. Quastech take special care of the candidates when it comes to placement. We train them not only to face the Technical interview but also for the HR interview. Face to face discussion is done with every candidate before they go for interview.

Placement for the QUASTECH candidate

Placement Process is very simple and Transparent for QUASTECH candidate :

Placement Starts in mid of the training after the mock test is cleared by the candidate, which is conducted during their training.

Separate session will be conducted for the candidates to explain the placement procedure.

  • Candidate will receive information on registered number regarding job description.
  • If any confusion candidate can get in touch with our placements support team.
  • Candidate need to apply for job, if interested.
  • After short-listing one to one discussion will be done with candidate regarding the interview schedule.
  • Candidate will attend the interview as per the given schedule.

Non QUASTECH candidate

  • As we have tie up with various IT companies so QUASTECH also help the outsider candidate to get placed. Placement for the external Candidate will be free of cost.
  • To start their placement candidate can either visit our Thane and Borivali Branch with their updated resume between 11 am to 5 pm (Monday to Saturday) OR they can send their updated resume on placement@quastech.in After which our placement team will call the candidate for the further process.
  • FREE Placement Support for Outsider Candidate, who have done any Certification Course related to IT from any other institute.
